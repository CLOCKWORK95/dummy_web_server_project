Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 table spoons of cocoa powder
  • 100 ml of milk
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat the milk at microwaves
  2. Put cinnamon and the two spoons of cocoa powder and shake
  3. Add sugar (optional)

Brownies

Ingredients

  • 50g of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g of brown sugar
  • 150gr of chocolate
  • 100ml of milk
  • 200gr of white wheat flour
  • 200gr *of butter

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Put in the oven at 150° for about half an hour or little more
  3. Take it out and cut in regular squares
  4. Cover the brownies with some sugar (optional)

Chocolate cake

Ingredients

  • 2 table spoons of cocoa powder
  • 100 ml of milk
  • 250gr of wheat flour
  • 120gr of brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • Cherry/apricot jam
  • Cinnamon
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Heat the milk at microwaves
  2. Put cinnamon and the two spoons of cocoa powder and shake
  3. Add sugar and flour and mix everything together
  4. Add yeast before putting in the oven at 200° for about 40 minutes.
  5. Take out and make it refresh before cutting and serving it.
